Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform.

How does the scoring system work?

We use a dual engine: first, a strict rule-based parser tests standard layout rules (contact info, sections, active verbs, numeric impact markers). Second, a generative AI model reviews semantic quality, experience depth, and formatting clarity to give you recruiter-level suggestions.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes. All uploads are processed strictly in memory and saved files are scrubbed from our systems immediately after parsing. We do not store or sell any resumes or personal information.

What formats do you support?

We fully support PDF (.pdf) and Microsoft Word (.docx) files, which are the standard formats accepted by modern applicant tracking systems.

Can I target specific job postings?

Absolutely. When uploading, paste the target job description in the optional form. The dashboard will analyze specific keyword overlaps, highlight missing skills, and calculate a tailored job match index.
